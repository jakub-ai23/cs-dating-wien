## Edition-by-edition evolution

### Edition I — 22 June 2026 (Vienna, 1st district)
- 20 spots (10 + 10)
- €39 first-edition price (€69 standard)
- Mystery venue revealed 48 h before to confirmed guests
- **Photos:** taken at event. May be used for marketing (social, website, next-edition promo). Soft consent — any attendee can request removal by email.
- Manual review by Jakub + Martina
- Goal: prove the format, generate first photo+testimonial assets

### Edition II — date TBA (after Edition I retrospective)
- **No photos policy as a feature.** Selling point: "discretion is part of the product." Strong differentiator vs. typical event marketing. Attendees relax knowing nothing ends up on Instagram.
  - Decision: 2026-05-17
  - Rationale: the kind of committed people we want (people serious about meeting someone) value privacy. Tire-kickers want the photo op. Filtering by photo-policy filters by intent.
- Price: re-evaluate after Edition I (probably move to standard €69)
- Same 20-spot cap until demand consistently exceeds supply

### Edition III+ — open
- Decision point after Editions I + II data: scale (more spots, multiple cities), or stay tight (20 spots, premium positioning)
- Slavic expansion (slavicdating.eu defensive park) — option held, not committed

## Positioning principles

- **Quality over volume.** 20 committed > 40 curious.
- **No tire-kickers.** Cancellation policy is sharp inside 7 days. No "I'll check and leave" refund.
- **Slovak-primary brand.** CZ + EN are mirrors, not equal markets. Vienna SK/CZ community is the wedge.
- **Founder visible.** Jakub + Martina are named, photographed, accessible. No hiding behind a brand.

---

## Open expansion options (parked, not active)

- **slavicdating.eu** — domain held. Potential expansion to Polish, Ukrainian, Croatian community in Vienna if Edition I+II prove demand exists.
- **Other cities** — Prague, Bratislava, Berlin. Not before Edition III in Vienna.
- **Premium tier** — small private dinners (6–8 people, screened harder, higher price). Not before Edition II data.
