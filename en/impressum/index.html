Nature of the event

Czech-Slovak Dating Vienna is a private, informal gathering for a limited circle of invited guests from the Slovak and Czech community in Vienna. It is not a commercial dating service, nor a public event.

Participation is possible only on the basis of an individual application and confirmation by the organiser.

Participant contribution

The standard participant contribution is €69. Until 1 June 2026, the code EDITION1 is available, reducing the contribution by €30 and including a welcome drink (final amount €39). The contribution is voluntary, covers the costs associated with organising the gathering (venue booking, refreshments, coordination), and is not a payment for services in the sense of a commercial relationship.

The contribution is payable online via the Stripe payment gateway. Cancellation conditions and refunds are described in detail in the document Terms of participation.

Venue and time

Gatherings take place in Vienna. The exact address is communicated to confirmed guests no later than 48 hours before the event.
