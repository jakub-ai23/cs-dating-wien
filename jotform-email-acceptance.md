## ⚠️ BLOCKER — placeholders to fill before sending

Replace BEFORE you send the first one:
- `[VENUE NAME]` → e.g., `Wein & Co Stephansplatz`
- `[ADRESA]` → e.g., `Jasomirgottstraße 3-5, 1010 Wien`
- `[U-Bahn/tram info]` → e.g., `U1/U3 Stephansplatz, 2 min pešo`

Once you have these three things, this email is shippable.
