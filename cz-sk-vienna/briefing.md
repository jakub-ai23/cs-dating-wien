## The Concept

Premium speed dating evenings in Vienna for **Slovaks and Czechs, 25–39**, looking for a serious partner.

**The hook is not nationality. The hook is shared interior life:**
- Same mother tongue (mutually intelligible SK/CZ)
- Same cultural references (childhood TV, food, holidays, family rhythms)
- Same immigrant journey in Vienna (the "I live here, but I dream in another language" experience)
- Same generation of Eastern/Central European 25–39s building lives abroad

This is the audience that says: "Po nemecky to ide. Ale niekoho, kto rozumie, hľadám stále."

## Why now / why this works

- Vienna has a large, professional Slovak + Czech diaspora — both communities have been here in waves since EU enlargement (2004) and continue growing.
- Apps don't filter for shared cultural background in any meaningful way.
- General Vienna speed dating is German-language and culturally Austrian — fine for connection, not for someone who wants to share a life with someone whose grandma also made bryndzové halušky / svíčková.
- The 25–39 band is the "build a life / find a partner" decade. Higher intent than older or younger cohorts.
- Same playbook as Inner Circle Wien, validated demand pattern: profession-or-identity-filtered premium events fill a real gap in Vienna.

## Positioning

**Premium, not exclusive-snob. Selective, but warm.** Partner search is the goal — this is not a casual social mixer. The tone is:
- "Conečne niekto, kto rozumie." — finally someone who gets it
- Not "elite" — *kuratované*. Curated.
- Premium price (€89 default, swap if needed) signals seriousness on both sides.

## Target — sharper

- **Age:** 25–39 (we may split into 25–32 and 32–39 once we have volume)
- **Profession:** Open. The filter is language + cultural roots, not profession (unlike Inner Circle).
- **Years in Vienna:** Doesn't matter. Recent arrivals through 10+ years all welcome.
- **Goal:** Serious partner search. Not networking, not friendship-only, not casual hookups. Application questionnaire screens for intent.

## Format (default — copied from Inner Circle)

- 30 places per event (15+15)
- One evening, ~3 hours including warm-up
- Moderated speed-dating rounds + opening ritual + drinks
- Premium location, 1st district Vienna
- €89 ticket
- Selective: applicants fill a short questionnaire, organizer curates the room

Ice-breakers: pull 2–3 from `inner-circle-wien/energizers-report.md` — *The Polarizing Statement*, *The Inner Circle Card*, *The Host Disarms First* all transfer cleanly. Bonus: add a culturally-anchored polarizing statement set ("Po koľkých rokoch vo Viedni si ešte Slovák / Čech?", "Vianoce slávim ešte stále po našom").
